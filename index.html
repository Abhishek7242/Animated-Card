Iron Man

Tony Stark, a brilliant industrialist, constructs a high-tech armored suit after being captured. Escaping with the suit, he decides to use his advanced technology to combat evil forces and protect the world, becoming the superhero known as Iron Man.

Thanos

Thanos, a powerful warlord from Titan, seeks to collect all six Infinity Stones. With these stones, he aims to wield ultimate power to wipe out half of all life in the universe, believing it will bring balance and prevent overpopulation.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, a frail young man, volunteers for an experimental program that transforms him into the super-soldier Captain America. Armed with his indestructible shield, he fights against tyranny and evil, becoming a symbol of hope and justice during World War II.
